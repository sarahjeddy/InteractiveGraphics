GameBoy Scene: Ghost Farmer

This was This was a collaborative game I made with Karissa in GB Studio as a way to explore the different possibilties of games that can be made with GameBoy techonlogy.

The hardest part of this project for me was navigating how to make a fuctional game with the constraints that exist in GameBoy. Another difficulty was figuring out how best to use the limited number of color palettes.

Overall, this project really helped me get a feel for both GB Studio and the GameBoy.
